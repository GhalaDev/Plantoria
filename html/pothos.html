Indoor • Easy care

Pothos

45 Riyal Light: Low to medium
Description
Pothos is a fast-growing vine that thrives in many conditions. It’s very forgiving and perfect for beginners.
Watering
Water every 7–10 days, allowing the soil to dry slightly between waterings.
Lighting
Low to medium indirect light. It can also adapt to brighter areas if kept away from direct sun.
