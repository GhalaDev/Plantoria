Outdoor • Fragrant

Plumeria

95 Riyal Light: Strong sun
Description
Plumeria, also known as Frangipani, is famous for its fragrant white and yellow flowers. It’s often used in gardens and around entrances.
Watering
Water every 5–7 days during the growing season. Reduce watering in winter and avoid leaving the soil soggy.
Lighting
Needs strong direct sunlight for good flowering. Place it in an open outdoor area with plenty of light.
