Indoor • Easy care

Monstera

85 Riyal Light: Bright, filtered
Description
Monstera is a tropical plant known for its large, split leaves. It adds a lush, dramatic look to any room.
Watering
Once every 7 days, letting the top of the soil dry slightly between waterings.
Lighting
Bright, filtered light such as window light or artificial room lighting. Avoid strong direct sun.
