Shefflera

Description: Schefflera, also known as the Umbrella Plant, has glossy leaves that fan out like an umbrella. It’s a great decorative plant that’s easy to maintain.

Watering: Water every 7–9 days; keep the soil slightly moist but not soggy.

Lighting: Bright, indirect sunlight, can tolerate some direct light.

Price: 110 Riyal
