Indoor • Moderate care

Schefflera

110 Riyal Light: Bright, indirect
Description
Schefflera, also known as the Umbrella Plant, has glossy leaves that fan out like an umbrella. It’s a great decorative plant that’s easy to maintain.
Watering
Water every 7–9 days; keep the soil slightly moist but never soggy.
Lighting
Bright, indirect sunlight. It can tolerate some gentle direct light in the morning or late afternoon.
