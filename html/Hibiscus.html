Hibiscus

90 Riyal Light: Full sun
Description
Hibiscus is a bright, tropical plant with large colorful flowers. It adds a vibrant touch to balconies and gardens.
Watering
Water every 3–5 days, keeping the soil slightly moist, especially in hot weather.
Lighting
Prefers full sunlight for most of the day. It can handle light afternoon shade in very hot areas.
