Outdoor • Climbing

Bougainvillea

75 Riyal Light: Full sun
Description
Bougainvillea is a strong climbing plant with bright, papery bracts. It covers walls and fences with rich color.
Watering
Water every 7–10 days. Allow the soil to dry slightly between waterings; too much water reduces flowering.
Lighting
Requires full direct sun to bloom well. Best placed in open outdoor spots that receive several hours of sunlight.
